Our commitment to responsible practices

  • We maintain appropriate security measures to protect information
  • Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill service requirements

SMS Consent and Messaging

When you provide your phone number and opt in to receive SMS messages from AI Automation Services, you consent to the following:

What You're Consenting To

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages from AI Automation Services related to service coordination, scheduling, confirmations, and updates. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply.

Types of Messages

All messages we send are transactional or conversational in nature. This includes:

  • Responses to user inquiries
  • Appointment confirmations and scheduling updates
  • Service-related notifications
  • Support communications

No Marketing Messages

We do not send marketing, promotional, or advertising messages. Our communications are limited to responding to user requests and providing service-related information.

Phone Number Privacy

We do not sell, share, or rent your phone number to third parties for marketing purposes. Your phone number is used solely for the transactional messaging purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance. Once you opt out, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us unless you opt in again.

To opt in to SMS messages, visit our SMS Opt-In page.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to opt out of messages at any time.

Information you provide is used only for the following purposes:
